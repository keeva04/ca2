Boone Marston

Boone Marston, a cybernetically enhanced outlaw, is considered extremely dangerous and highly unpredictable. Last confirmed sighting was in Sector 9's undercity, where he outmaneuvered authorities with alarming speed. Approach with extreme caution; capture bounty: 150,000 credits alive.

Official Cargo Loss Report - Case #3927-B

Date Filed: April 14, 2095
Filed By: Captain Elys Vorn, Sector 7 Logistics Authority

On April 13, 2095, at approximately 02:43 local time, an unauthorized breach occurred aboard Transport Vessel AX-9, en route from New Terra Port to Omega Exchange. The stolen cargo comprised classified tech modules, including encryption processors, kinetic dampeners, and experimental neurolink parts. Surveillance logs reveal unidentified hostiles using cloaking devices to bypass detection. No casualties reported; however, three security drones were dismantled. Estimated value of losses: 3.2 million credits. Investigation underway; Cipher Gang suspected. Immediate recovery operations authorized by the Central Coalition Security Council. Individuals with information leading to the retrieval of goods or capture of involved parties will receive up to 500,000 credits.

The Cipher Gang

The Cipher Gang is a shadowy collective specializing in digital warfare, corporate sabotage, and cyber-heists. Known for breaching high-security networks without leaving a trace, they have targeted government archives, private vaults, and interstellar banking systems. Their signature method involves siphoning data and assets while simultaneously launching deep-fake diversions. Core members are rumored to operate from a mobile command ship known only as 'The Ghost.' Current bounty for credible intelligence: 250,000 credits; for apprehension of gang leaders: 1 million credits. Priority alert status: Red. Public advised to report any unusual tech anomalies immediately.
